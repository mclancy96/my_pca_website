Some of My Projects

Runway with QA

I built this fork of Runway as a demonstration of what I see as a potential feature for Runway that will help improve the workflow of QA Ninjas. It creates two new models in Runway called Test Cases and Test Steps that are used to store and track the tests that Ninjas perform for stories. This allows for better accountability for Ninjas and a clearer communication around testing.

Power Org Chart Builder

I created this after finding issues with relationships between user_titles. I used PyGraphViz to create a graph of the relationships between the titles by making connections with the managed_by_title_id column. This helped us to find titles and departments that had been orphaned. We even found that one title was its own manager.

Jam Sessions

Jam Sessions was a project that myself and two other students worked on in my computer science capstone class. The website is designed to help musicians find other musicians that wanted to make music together or play together. We had regular scrum meetings and biweekly sprint planning to stay on top of our progress. We used Trello to organize our project. It was built using React on the front end and Node on the backend. We used a relational database for storing data.
